About GET Follow

GET Follow offers real time tracking of field employees which is a feature that you can see the live location of all the employees, track their progress and check if they are visiting all the required customers assigned to them or not.

Attendance

This App has to maintain Field force attendance with Punch IN and Punch OUT time.

Real time Location

The real time location tracking ensures complete transparency between field force and supervisor. Get Follow app allow you to see and manage your crew across multiple locations from a single dashboard.

Location History

Get Follow keeps record of the locations your employee has visited and you can check the record anytime.
